Courses

This is an overview of the programs offered at TICS:

Computer Packages

Our main focus is on providing high-quality fundamental computer application with a clear industrial approach. Including MS Office Suite; - Word Processing and Artistic applications

Advanced Design Applications

We provide exhaustive approach to computer design applications including; - Adobe Suite, Statistical applications and Computerised Accounting packages.

Computer Engineering

We have a dedicated technical team is providing a comprehensive approach in Computer Engineering packages. This include: - Computer Networking, PC Maintenace and CAD systems.

What we are ?

Tilas Institute of Computer Studies is a private computer training institution established in 2018. We are a registered Business computer training institute under the Act of (Cap, 499, section 14). We offer certificate and diploma training in all computer related skills & provide a place for Students attachments. We are partnering with International Education and Recourse Network (iEARN).
